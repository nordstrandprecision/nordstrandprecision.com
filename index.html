At Nordstrand, we're here for the hard stuff.



Our Capabilities Include:

  • Precision Machining

    From high-mix prototyping to quick-turnaround production, we focus on achieving and maintaining tight tolerances.

  • Quality Assurance

    You can't make what you can't measure. All of our parts go through rigorous measurement inspection plans to ensure you're getting above and beyond what you require.

  • Design For Manufacturing

    Manufacturing isn't a solitary science, and we believe in strong two-way communication for design and manufacturing to fabricate with function in mind.

  • Process Analysis

    Consistent and trackable process stability is key when working with high product mixes, and those statistics are available to our customers to guarantee great quality.

  • Engineering Consulting

    Need a second opinion? We're happy to offer our insight and assist with your project.

  • LEAN Manufacturing

    Our machine shop is designed to take advantage of the latest advancements in technology and enable us to work safer, faster, and more efficient.
